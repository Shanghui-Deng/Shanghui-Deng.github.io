I am currently a PhD candidate at the School of [Computer Science](https://cs.cug.edu.cn/), [China University of Geosciences (Wuhan)](https://www.cug.edu.cn/), advised by [Prof.Tang Chang](https://tangchang.net/). I got my M.S. degree in Computer Technology from [Computer Science](https://cs.mnnu.edu.cn/), [Minnan Normal University](https://www.mnnu.edu.cn/), supervised by [Prof.Jingmin Yang](https://cs.mnnu.edu.cn/info/1070/1234.htm). My research interests include machine learning and indoor localization, especially multi-view/multi-modal representation learning and multi-view clustering.
